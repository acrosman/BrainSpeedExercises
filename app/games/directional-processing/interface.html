Directional Processing

How to Play

A moving pattern will appear briefly on screen. Your task is to identify which direction it moves — Up, Down, Left, or Right.

  1. Watch the pattern carefully — it will only appear for a short time.
  2. After it stops use the on-screen buttons or arrow keys to indicate which direction it moved.
  3. Get 3 correct in a row to advance to a harder level.
  4. Get 3 wrong in a row to drop back 2 levels.

As you advance, the pattern becomes harder to see: shorter display time and lower contrast force your visual cortex to work faster.
