How to Play

Watch which circles briefly highlight — those are your targets.

  1. Observe the highlighted circles during the marking phase.
  2. Keep tracking the same circles as all move around the arena.
  3. When movement stops, click each circle you believe was a target.
  4. After selecting all targets the round is scored automatically.
