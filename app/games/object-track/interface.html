How to Play

Your goal is to track the selected balls as they move around the arena. Once they start to move all the balls are the same so watch which circles briefly highlight — those are your targets.

  1. Observe the highlighted balls at the beginning of each round.
  2. Keep tracking the same ball as all move around the arena.
  3. When movement stops, click each ball you believe was a target.
  4. After selecting three targets the round is scored automatically.
  5. As the game progresses, the number of balls and their speed will increase.
