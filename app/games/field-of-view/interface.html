Field of View

How to Play

A kitten appears in the center while a toy appears on an outer-edge cell. After a brief flash, the field image masks the board.

  1. Memorize which kitten appeared in the center.
  2. Memorize where the toy appeared in the periphery (outer edge).
  3. After the field mask, select the kitten and click the toy location.
  4. The game adapts your display time with a 1-up / 2-down staircase.
