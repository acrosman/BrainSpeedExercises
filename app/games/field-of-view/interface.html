Field of View

How to Play

Phase 1 and 2 happen together: a kitten appears in the center while a toy appears on an outer-edge cell. After a brief flash, the field image masks the board.

  1. Memorize which kitten appeared in the center.
  2. Memorize where the toy appeared in the periphery (outer edge).
  3. After the field mask, select the kitten and click the toy location.
  4. The game adapts your SOA with a 1-up / 2-down staircase.

Image Assets In Use

  • primaryKitten.png (center option 1)
  • secondaryKitten.png (center option 2)
  • toy1.png and toy2.png (peripheral targets)
  • Field.png (full-field visual mask)
