Sound Sweep

How to Play

You will hear two rapid frequency sweeps in quick succession. Each sweep either goes Up (low pitch to high, like a "weep") or Down (high pitch to low, like a "woop").

  1. Listen carefully to both sweeps.
  2. Identify the sequence you heard: Up-Up, Up-Down, Down-Up, or Down-Down.
  3. Use the buttons or press keys 1–4 to select your answer.
  4. Get 3 correct in a row to advance to a harder (faster) level.
  5. Get 3 wrong in a row to drop back 2 levels.

As you advance, the sweeps become shorter and the gap between them shrinks. Eventually the sounds become very brief chirps that challenge your brain to process fast auditory transitions.
