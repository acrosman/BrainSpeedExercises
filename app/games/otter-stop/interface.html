Otter Stop!

How to Play

A stream of otters needs your help — but one sneaky otter means STOP!

  • Otters appear one at a time in rapid succession.
  • Press Space or click the image for every otter — you must respond or it counts as a miss!
  • When the sneaky fish appears, do nothing — freeze!
  • The game speeds up as your accuracy improves. Good luck!
