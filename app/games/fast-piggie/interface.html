Fast Piggie

How to Play

A set of guinea pigs spread around a circle will flash on screen briefly. Only one will look like the image below. Select that guinea pig in each round.

  • Watch the game play area carefully when it appears.
  • After the piggies vanish, click the wedge where the orange one was hiding.
  • Keyboard users can use arrow keys to navigate wedges and press Enter or Space to confirm.
  • Rounds get harder (more piggies and less time) as you progress.
