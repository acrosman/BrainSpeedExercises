Card Rat

How to Play

Cards are dealt rapidly. React only when two cards in a row share the same value, or when a joker appears.

  • Press Space or click the card area when you see a valid slap target.
  • Do not react on non-matching cards.
  • You must react before the next card is dealt.
  • Each correct reaction speeds up future deals.
